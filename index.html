// Main Feature
Progressive
Bid Wall

Automated buy-side price support, native to Flaunch.

Every single swap on $BABYAST accrues trading fees. As the community share of those fees reaches 0.1 ETH, Flaunch's Uniswap V4 hook automatically deploys that ETH as a bid just below the current spot price. The more the token trades, the taller the wall gets. When price rises, the wall rises with it. This is not manual. This is not discretionary. This is a smart contract, running forever.

Transparency Note

The Progressive Bid Wall is a protocol-level feature of Flaunch, implemented as a Uniswap V4 hook. The exact community-to-creator fee split is configured at launch and is fully visible on-chain. Our commitment is a community-majority split routed directly into the bid wall. No promises of a floor, no price guarantees, just a transparent on-chain mechanism that turns volume into buy pressure.
